Oasis is a gender-focused hackathon in Austin, Texas

June 8 FUTO HQ
A hackathon is a social coding marathon where teenagers come together to build projects for a weekend and share them with the world.
The Hack Club Days-of-Service initiative aims to help girls and gender minorities write their first lines of code. Partnering with Girls Scouts across the nation, the DOS initiative approaches Computer Science with beginner-friendly workshops.
